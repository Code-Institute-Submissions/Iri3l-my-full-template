Maintenance services

We can offer a large area of maintenance for you

What we can offer

Preventive maintenance:

Carried out in order to keep something in working order or extend its life, such as replacing cracked roofing tiles before inclement weather.

Corrective maintenance:

This involves repairing something that has broken, such as a window or guttering.

Front-line maintenance:

This involves maintaining something while it is still in use, such as repainting and decorating an occupied building.

Proactive maintenance:

Maintenance work that is undertaken to avoid failures or to identify defects that could lead to failure.

Reliability centred maintenance:

A combination of maintenance strategies used to ensure a physical asset continues to function correctly.
